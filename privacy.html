Who we are

Multicore Management provides back-office admin support for UK tradespeople. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information when you use our website or services.

What we collect

When you fill in our contact form or get in touch with us, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Any details you include in your message

How we use your information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Provide our admin support services
  • Keep in touch about your account if you become a client

We will never sell your information to third parties or use it for anything you wouldn't expect.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your cookie preference. We do not currently use analytics or tracking cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent.

How we store your data

Contact form submissions are sent to us via email through FormSubmit.co. We store client information securely and only keep it for as long as we need it to provide our services.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO if you are unhappy with how we handle your data
